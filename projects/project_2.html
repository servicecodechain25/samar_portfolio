Honoring the Spirit of Our Soldiers Through Running" The Adani Ahmedabad Marathon 2024 is not just a race; it's a movement to show solidarity and gratitude towards the brave soldiers who protect our nation. This year's campaign, #Run4Our Soldiers, is designed to inspire runners to dedicate their efforts to the courage, sacrifice, and resilience of our armed forces. The campaign employs an ilustration-based approach, symbolizing the energy, unity, and determination of the participants. The artwork depicts runners charging forward against a vibrant backdrop of Ahmedabad's iconic cityscape, with the Indian flag waving proudlly, representing the spirit of patrio tism and perseverance. The use of bold colors and dynamic figures creates a sense of motion and enthusiasm,encouraging people to join the cause.
